Vision

Our vision is to help student developers learn from and work with some of the most experienced and skilled developers in the country, as well as to give them the opportunity of interacting with new technologies that they otherwise might not have been familiar with. We want to create a space that nurtures and encourages students to let their creative juices flow freely and engage in lively discussions with each other, which may sow the seeds for some truly revolutionary ideas. We hope that this initiative will make a significant contribution towards using development to solve real world problems.
